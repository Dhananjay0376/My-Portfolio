## Architecture & Tech Stack Decisions

### Architecture
- **Framework**: Next.js 15 (App Router)
- **Language**: TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS + shadcn/ui + framer-motion

### Specific Libraries
- **WebGL/3D**: @react-three/fiber, @react-three/drei, three
- **AI**: ai (Vercel AI SDK), @supabase/supabase-js
- **Content**: next-mdx-remote or @next/mdx
